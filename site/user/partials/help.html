Help

This is the Edmonds School District's 6898 Emergency Reporting Application.
This will record submitted school-related incidents and automatically contact the proper district employees.
To report an incident, just fill out the form under the "New Incident" tab (you may leave comments blank), and submit it.
This will record the information in our database and notify the required personnel.
You may also search through the incident records under the "Search Incidents" tab, using a variety of search parameters.
You can leave certain parameters blank to not use them, or leave them all blank to pull all records.
If you have any comments, questions, or concerns, feel free to contact
Thank you.
