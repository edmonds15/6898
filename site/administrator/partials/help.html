Help

This is the Edmonds School District 6898 Administrator Page.
This page gives you administrator controls over the 6898 incident reporting page.
Under Change Users, you can add a new site user by filling out the Add New User form with their login username and application role.
You can also Edit an existing user's username or role, and delete a user.
Under Change Incidents, you can change a previously recorded incident by searching for and then clicking on it.
Once the information is shown, you can click on edit to edit the information or delete to delete it.
Note that the incident number, date created, and creator information cannot be edited.
If you have any comments, questions, or concerns, feel free to contact.
Thank you.
